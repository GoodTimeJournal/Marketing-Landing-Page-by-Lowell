Apply professional design principles to your own life

Keep an activity log

Record your activities each day along with an assessment of how engaged and excited you were during each undertaking. Eventually you'll have a clear picture of what brings you the most joy!

Keep a reflection journal

Spend time each week reflecting over what you've learned from your activity logs. Consistent introspection and written insights will allow you to detect patterns in your life you can use as design inspiration!

Learn more about your internal values

Keeping your Good Time Journal and thoughtfully recording your impressions about your activities will reveal which pursuits in your life align best with your internal goals. Your path forward to designing your own life to maximize joy and fulfillment will become more clear.

Good Times Journal is a tool to help you design your life

Our mission is to help people discover their values and give people tools from the design world to structure their lives to find the most meaning and joy possible.
